Welcome to Our Organic Buffalo Farm

At Karthik's Buffalo Dairy, we take pride in raising healthy, happy buffaloes that produce the finest quality milk. Our farm follows sustainable practices and provides natural, hormone-free dairy products to our community.

With over 50 Murrah buffaloes, we're one of the leading dairy farms in the region, offering fresh milk, ghee, paneer, and other dairy products.

Our Products & Services

About Our Farm

Established in 2015, Karthik's Buffalo Dairy started with just 5 buffaloes and a dream to provide pure, unadulterated milk to our local community. Today, we've grown into a model dairy farm with state-of-the-art facilities while maintaining our commitment to ethical farming practices.

Our farm spans 10 acres of lush green pastures where our buffaloes graze freely. We use modern milking equipment while ensuring each animal receives individual care and attention.

All our products are made fresh daily without any preservatives or additives. We follow strict hygiene standards at every step from milking to packaging.
